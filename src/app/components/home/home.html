Uniqueness Analysis

Unlock the power of understanding logical contributions with Polari's Uniqueness and Contribution Analysis feature. Utilizing Logic Democratization, we generate a user-defined coherent general logic model across all fields for subjects that are analyzable. We then use AI and web-crawling, together with user inputs, to break down the abstraction-context pairs. The most commonly used and agreed upon logically coherent and efficient models serve as the central point for the concept, going to the lowest level concepts possible and iterating to form a coherent mapping of the entire system.

We then date the earliest identifiable mentions of those concepts and isolate those within a range of time where it is reasonable or plausible they were developed independently but arrived at the same conclusion. This creates a static mapping of all logic in the system to their historical discovery while abstracting together logical equivalents. When combined with Political Logic Democratization implementation, we can add weights to how important particular parts of the contribution are in given scenarios, to create a model by which we can determine the fairness of a distribution of profits based on contribution according to organizations and individuals as contributors.
